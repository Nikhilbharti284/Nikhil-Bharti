Stay Safe, Stay Healthy

COVID-19 is a contagious disease caused by the coronavirus. Taking preventive measures can significantly reduce the risk of infection and spread.

  • Wear a mask in crowded places.
  • Maintain social distancing (6 feet).

Cancer Awareness

Early Detection Saves Lives

Stay Informed, Stay Healthy

Cancer is a group of diseases characterized by uncontrolled cell growth. Early diagnosis and preventive measures can help improve survival rates.

  • Go for regular health screenings.
  • Maintain a healthy diet and exercise regularly.
  • Avoid tobacco and limit alcohol consumption.
  • Protect yourself from harmful UV rays.
  • Get vaccinated for HPV and Hepatitis B.

Health Emergency Awareness

Stay Prepared, Stay Safe

Know What To Do

Health emergencies can happen anytime. Knowing how to respond can save lives.

  • Learn basic first aid.
  • Keep emergency contacts handy.
  • Know CPR techniques.
  • Stay calm in crisis situations.
